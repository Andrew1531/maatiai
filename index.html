Our Guiding Principle

Empowering Communities Through AI Self-Determination

At MIAI, our mission is to dismantle the monopolization of AI technology by BigTech and replace superficial solutions with transformative, enduring technologies that truly serve the needs of marginalized communities.

We build literacy and competence with data and AI tools in marginalized communities. We want these communities to create their own solutions, maintain their intergenerational viability, and ensure their technological self-determination.

Inspired by Ma'at, the ancient Egyptian value system, MIAI envisions a future where marginalized communities, through their independent creation of data and AI technologies, are embodiments of truthfulness, equitable service, and life that honor the oneness of humanity.

Our Technical Services

Operational Excellence

We provide the infrastructure and expertise to transform raw information into clear, actionable insights for organizational growth.

Technology Optimization & Contract Negotiation

Technology is critical to your mission, but organizations often purchase ad-hoc technologies with redundant capabilities, leaving money on the table. We evaluate your current technology landscape and supply chain to help you reduce costs and maximize utility.

Our service includes analyzing current and general contracts to identify risks and create leverage for procurement. We also assist with software asset management to mitigate audit risks.

Customer Relationship Management System

While CRM systems are essential, they can easily become improperly used and convoluted. We offer comprehensive CRM optimization and strategy, including identifying the main use cases, ensuring optimal data storage, and automating redundant processes.

We connect external forms directly to your CRM to eliminate manual data entry, create new procedures based on your needs, and implement dashboards to summarize key data points.

Artificial Intelligence Integration

Artificial intelligence (AI) has become a powerhouse for enhancing organizational workflows. We provide AI system integrations and strategies by teaching your organization how to efficiently use AI to improve processes.

Our consulting services include system analysis-driven recommendations, implementation, and training to embed this powerful tool into your daily operations. We also host educational sessions for your organization.

Data Analysis & Visualization

Data analysis and visualization are key to enhancing your offerings, developing ideas for funding, developing data pipelines with your CRMs and creating operational efficiencies.

We transform your raw information into clear, actionable insights through information management and impact tracking systems. This includes facilitating complex data migration processes and developing visual dashboards for real-time clarity.
