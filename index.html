Our Core Initiatives

We focus on three pillars of technology to ensure that the future of AI belongs to everyone.

Ethical AI Training

Providing marginalized communities with the knowledge to build, audit, and understand AI systems without corporate bias.

Learn More

Sustainable Tech

Developing low-compute AI models that can run on affordable hardware in regions with limited power infrastructure.

Learn More

Open Data Justice

Advocating for data privacy rights and creating open-source datasets that represent diverse global languages and cultures.

Why Maati AI exists.

The "Maati" (Soil) represents our foundation. Just as healthy soil is required for life, a healthy ethical foundation is required for the survival of human agency in the age of AI.

  • Human-Centric Design: Every line of code we write is evaluated for social impact first.
  • Radical Transparency: All our funding, research, and models are available for public audit.
  • Global Community: We work with grassroots organizers in over 15 countries.
